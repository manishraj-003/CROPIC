# Pilot District Plan

## Pilot Candidates
- Haryana: Karnal, Hisar
- Madhya Pradesh: Sehore, Vidisha

## Selection Criteria
- Crop diversity across Kharif and Rabi cycles
- Existing PMFBY claim volume
- Mixed connectivity conditions
- Local FLW availability for pilot operations

## AOI Deliverables
- District polygon GeoJSON files
- Farm sample grid for baseline coverage
- Sentinel-2 query windows by season
